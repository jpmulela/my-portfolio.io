Basic Financials: Introduction to the Facility Operating Statement

 

This virtual offering is designed to familiarize associates with the layout of the Retail Facility Operating Statement. Associates will gain a better understanding of the key metrics of sales, gross profit, controllable and non-controllable expenses, direct operating profit, and inventory statistics as they relate to the Operating Statement.

 

Competencies this course enhances:


  • Apply business and financial acumen
  • Analyze problems and make sound decisions
  • Adapt and learn
