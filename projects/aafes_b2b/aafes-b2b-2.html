As a forward thinking organization, the Exchange recognizes that an indispensable mix in a Total Rewards program includes Tuition Assistance which strengthens an organization’s ability to attract, motivate and retain talent.

We are excited to announce we are accepting applications for the Tuition Assistance program! The Tuition Assistance program supports the Exchange’s strategic priority to develop and inspire future leaders. Participation in the Tuition Assistance program allows associates to complete coursework at an accredited College or University to earn an undergraduate or graduate degree. Admission into the Tuition Assistance program is highly competitive. To learn more about the program and how to submit your application, please click here.

Applications must be submitted by 21 Aug 2019.

E-mail the Tuition Assistance Program Administrator if you have any questions.
